# 18 — Weekly Tracker

> Your weekly operating rhythm. Fill it each week; review every **Sunday**. The point isn't to look busy — it's to catch drift early (skipping system design, no mocks scheduled, referrals going cold).

**Target load:** 2 hrs/weekday + 4 hrs/weekend ≈ **~18 hrs/week**. Adjust honestly; consistency beats heroics.

## Anti-drift rules (the failure modes to watch)
- **No "all coding, no design."** Senior loops weight design heavily — keep both moving every week.
- **No mock gaps after Week 4.** A week with zero mocks scheduled is a red flag.
- **No cold referral pipelines.** If a contact's gone quiet 2 weeks, follow up or find another.
- **Protect sustainability.** If energy hits 1–2 for two weeks running, cut volume, not consistency — burnout mid-search is the real risk.
