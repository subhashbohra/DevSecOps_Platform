# Outreach Templates (referrals & recruiters)

Full versions with context in ../16-Referral-Strategy.md. Quick copies:

## Reconnect
> Hi [Name] — it's been a while! I've been following [specific]. I'm exploring senior platform/infra roles and [Company] is high on my list. 15 min to catch up this week?

## Referral ask
> Hi [Name] — I'm applying for [Role + link] at [Company] ([level]). Given my work on [1-line scope], I think it's a strong fit. Open to referring me or introducing me to the hiring manager? Resume attached — happy to make it easy.

## Recruiter reply
> Thanks [Name]. Quick context: I lead platform engineering at a global bank (lakehouse on GKE/GDC; air-gapped AI RCA platform approved bank-wide). Targeting [level]. Could you share level, team, and comp band?

## Thank-you (always close the loop)
> Thanks so much for the referral/intro, [Name] — really appreciate it. I'll keep you posted, and let me know if I can ever return the favor.
