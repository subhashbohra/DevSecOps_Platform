# 14 — Leadership Principles

> Primarily for the **AWS loop** (`08`), where every round is scored against Amazon's Leadership Principles by a Bar Raiser. Also a useful behavioral backbone for any company. Memorize the LPs, bank 2 stories each, and tag them to your `12` STAR stories.

---

## The 16 Amazon Leadership Principles

For each: the principle, what they're really testing, the question form, and your best story.

### 1. Customer Obsession
*Start with the customer and work backward.* They test: do you optimize for customer outcomes over internal convenience? **Q:** "Tell me about going above and beyond for a customer." **Story:** Cost Savings (6) / AI Platform user value (5).

### 2. Ownership
*Think long-term; act on behalf of the whole company; never "not my job."* **Q:** "Tell me about taking ownership beyond your role." **Story:** GitHub Migration (1), Production Incident (7).

### 3. Invent and Simplify
*Seek new ideas; simplify.* **Q:** "Tell me about a time you simplified something complex." **Story:** Config-driven migration framework (3), AI Platform (5).

### 4. Are Right, A Lot
*Strong judgment; seek diverse views; disconfirm your own beliefs.* **Q:** "Tell me about a decision others disagreed with that you got right." **Story:** Stakeholder Conflict (8).

### 5. Learn and Be Curious
*Always learning; explore new possibilities.* **Q:** "How do you stay current?" **Story:** AWS Community Builder (4).

### 6. Hire and Develop the Best
*Raise the bar; develop others; mentor.* **Q:** "Tell me about developing someone." **Story:** Mentoring (9).

### 7. Insist on the Highest Standards
*Relentlessly high bar; many won't think it's reasonable.* **Q:** "Tell me about holding a high standard under pressure." **Story:** Engineering Excellence (3), GitHub Migration security (1).

### 8. Think Big
*Bold direction that inspires results.* **Q:** "Tell me about a bold bet or big vision you drove." **Story:** AI Platform → bank-wide (5), OpenShift→GCP (2).

### 9. Bias for Action
*Speed matters; many decisions are reversible.* **Q:** "Tell me about a fast decision with incomplete info." **Story:** Production Incident (7), GitHub Migration (1).

### 10. Frugality
*Do more with less; constraints breed resourcefulness.* **Q:** "Tell me about achieving more with fewer resources." **Story:** Cost Savings (6).

### 11. Earn Trust
*Listen, speak candidly, treat others respectfully, be self-critical.* **Q:** "Tell me about earning trust / a time you were self-critical." **Story:** Stakeholder Conflict (8), Production Incident postmortem (7).

### 12. Dive Deep
*Operate at all levels; no detail beneath you; audit frequently.* **Q:** "Tell me about the deepest you've gone to solve a problem." **Story:** AI Platform internals (5), Production Incident RCA (7). *(Expect 5 layers of follow-up.)*

### 13. Have Backbone; Disagree and Commit
*Challenge respectfully even when uncomfortable; then commit fully.* **Q:** "Tell me about a time you disagreed and committed." **Story:** Stakeholder Conflict (8).

### 14. Deliver Results
*Focus on key inputs; deliver with quality, on time.* **Q:** "Tell me about delivering a hard result." **Story:** OpenShift→GCP (2), Engineering Excellence (3).

### 15. Strive to be Earth's Best Employer
*Safer, more productive, empathetic work environment; develop people.* **Q:** "How do you make your team better/healthier?" **Story:** Mentoring (9).

### 16. Success and Scale Bring Broad Responsibility
*Be thoughtful about broader societal/operational impact.* **Q:** "Tell me about considering second-order/broader consequences." **Story:** Regulated-bank security/compliance decisions (1, 2, 5).

## Cross-company leadership mapping

You don't re-learn behavioral prep per company — you re-tag the same stories.

| Theme | Amazon LP | Google (Googleyness) | Atlassian value |
|-------|-----------|----------------------|-----------------|
| Customer/user focus | Customer Obsession | User/mission focus | Don't #@!% the customer |
| Candor/disagreement | Have Backbone; D&C | Intellectual humility | Open company, no bullshit |
| Teamwork/low ego | Earn Trust | Collaboration | Play, as a team |
| Initiative | Bias for Action / Ownership | Conscientiousness | Be the change you seek |
| Quality/craft | Highest Standards | — | Build with heart and balance |
| Developing people | Hire & Develop | Collaboration | Play, as a team |

> Prep the **stories** once (`12`); re-tag to whichever framework the company uses.

## The "Dive Deep" drill (AWS-specific, but builds depth everywhere)

Pick a story. Have someone (or you) ask "why?" / "how exactly?" **five times in a row.** If you run out of real detail before five, that story isn't deep enough for an AWS senior loop — pick one you genuinely owned to the metal.

- [ ] I have ≥3 stories that survive 5 layers of "why/how."
