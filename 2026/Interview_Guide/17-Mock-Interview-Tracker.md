# 17 — Mock Interview Tracker

> Mocks are where prep becomes performance. Schedule them from Week 4. At your level, prioritize **system design + behavioral** mocks over coding. Log every one — patterns in your weaknesses only show up across mocks.

**Where to get mocks:** interviewing.io (paid, real FAANG interviewers, anonymous), Pramp (free peer), a trusted senior peer, or paid coaches. For behavioral, even a non-technical friend asking the questions + recording you is valuable.

**Cadence target:** 1/week from Week 4 → ≥10 total by Day 90 (mix per the plan in `19`).
